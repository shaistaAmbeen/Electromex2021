ABOUT US

Electromex Engineering Services is an MEP company based in Mumbai, India. We are a leading services provider in the field of mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and Fire Fighting design and execution work. We provide services for Residential Buildings like High-Rise, Residential Buildings & townships, Commercial Buildings like offices, studios, various Engineering Industries, Shopping Complexes, Laboratories & Banks, etc. in India and other countries.
